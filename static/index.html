Career Profile

I am a highly motivated system architect specialising in developing evolutionary architectures to help keep up with ever-changing business needs and technology.

Architecture is fundamental when developing systems, however, the best architecture will be almost invisible to the developer, allowing them the freedom to create functionality without restrictions imposed on them by architectural overlords, but with the safety of knowing that their code is secure, scalable, maintainable and changeable.

As an architect, my role is just as hands-on as a developer, with the added responsibility of setting a vision and embarking on the journey to get there by coaching, mentoring and empowering the teams around me. Architecture should never be set in stone from the start, it should evolve as the system, developers, technology, users and business evolve around it.

This is where my skills lie, in creating this evolutionary architecture and empowering those around me to utilise it for the best possible outcome.
